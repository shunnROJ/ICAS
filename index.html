ICAS Privacy Policy (Chatbot & App)

Updated: 8 September 2025

Who we are: ICAS is a disaster-management platform and chatbot that helps people report incidents (calamities and road accidents) and receive alerts.

What we collect

  • Incident details: incident type, description, time.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates and/or typed address (when you choose to share).
  • Media: photos/videos and related metadata (e.g., device timestamp).
  • Contact info (optional): name, phone, email for follow-up.
  • Technical data: app/chatbot identifiers, device info, IP address, logs, diagnostic data.
  • Usage data: interactions with chatbot/app features, timestamps.

How we use data

  • Create and route incident reports to responders.
  • Notify nearby users/responders (if alerts are enabled).
  • Verify, prioritize, and track incident response.
  • Improve safety features, quality, and reliability.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Consent (e.g., location sharing, media uploads).
  • Legitimate interests (service reliability, safety, analytics).
  • Legal obligation (law-enforcement requests, regulatory compliance).
  • Vital interests (protecting life/safety during emergencies).

Sharing & disclosure

  • Responders/authorities/authorized partners for emergency response.
  • Vendors (secure hosting, storage, analytics, communications).
  • Legal disclosures when required by law or to protect safety and rights.

We do not sell personal data.

Data retention

Incident records are retained per operational and legal requirements. Media and logs are retained only as long as necessary for response, auditing, and security. You can request deletion of your personal data where applicable (see “Your rights”).

Your choices & rights

  • Submit reports without contact info.
  • Toggle location sharing and withdraw consent where supported.
  • Depending on your jurisdiction: access, rectify, delete, restrict, port, or object to processing—contact us to exercise rights.

Children

ICAS is not intended for children under the age required by local law to consent to data processing. If we learn we processed such data, we will delete it.

Security

We use encryption in transit, access controls, logging, and least-privilege practices. No system is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

Cross-border transfers

Data may be processed and stored in countries different from yours, with safeguards as required by law (e.g., SCCs).

Offline/Free-Data uploads

When full internet is unavailable, the chatbot may use lightweight/free-data transport to submit photos and reports where supported by carriers/partners.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will change the “Updated” date and, where required, notify you.
